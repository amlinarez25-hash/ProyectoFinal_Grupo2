About Us


At Weekly Cups, our story began with four friends gathered around a small table, each holding a different drink in hand,from steaming coffee to sparkling tea blends. What started as casual conversations about flavors, traditions, and the memories tied to our favorite beverages soon turned into a shared passion to explore the world of drinks more deeply. We realized that every cup holds a story, it's history, it's benefits, and the joy it brings to people everywhere. Fueled by curiosity and creativity, we decided to create a space where we could share those stories with others. That’s how this blog was born: a weekly celebration of drinks, crafted with love and brewed with the idea that there’s always something new to discover.
